Nikita & Abhishek

9th May 2018
Mumbai, India
A Tale Of Engineers... Where Love Triumphed

Engineering is said to be the backbone of our country and very well it turned out to be the spine of this beautiful relationship as well.

Abhi was a typical north Indian brat who hogged up on every edible thing on earth and Niki was one of the high on heels sophisticated girls who would never talk to guys like him.

It was the second year of engineering, when the North and South clashed. Our Mr Glutton was immediately head over heels about our Ms Sophisticated.

Our Dadar boy was quick to figure out that Niki was from Matunga and realized that BEST could be the best way to inch closer to her.

It was an uphill battle for our Mr North but he ultimately managed to woo our beautiful Niki with his gentle attitude. She brought about the best in him and he made her smile.

They cherished all the highs and fought all the lows and more importantly, both became better individuals since then. And when you realize you want to spend the rest of your life with somebody, you want the rest of your life to start as soon as possible.

So, with love in our hearts and food in our minds we wish the lovely couple a happy married life. Grow old together and more importantly grow fat together.

The Bride -
Nikita Kanchan

A complete breath of fresh air with a pure, honest soul. Meet our bride to be, Nikita Kanchan aka Niki, an Engineer and now an established digital marketer.

A perfect combination of Carnatic Chiroti and Mumbai Masala, she has this cool air about her which leaves a lasting impression on the people she meets. Never ever has she been a drama queen to complain about anything and has rather blended like jaggery wherever she has gone, to add up on the sweetness.

No doubt Abhishek went head over heels after meeting her. Her sophistication gives a lot of complex to disorganized souls around her and maybe that’s why our boy seems on toes always.

The life of every gang has finally decided to tie the knot of togetherness with our dear Mumbaikar and we cannot be more excited about it!

The Groom -
Abhishek Bhalotia

Our groom to be Mr Abhishek Bhalotia, an Engineer by Education, is born and brought up in Mumbai itself. His super powers include his ability to hog on absolutely anything edible on earth and do wondersome analytics with data.

An ardent follower of cricket and a hardcore MI fan, he himself was a pretty good sportsman back during college. His calm demeanour, superlative sense of humour and unadultered soul makes him stand out of the rest.

Maybe that’s why Nikita our bride to be lost her heart to this super cool Mumbaikar!
